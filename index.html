Board

Recovery Mode

Light, Medium, or Heavy tiers simulate competition consequences — timed tactical decisions under fatigue.

How It Works

  1. Roll a mat scenario — choose your line.
  2. Watch the spectrum move — mat-heavy or board-ready.
  3. Solve a board decision — with time scaled to your state.
  4. Review the session summary — mat quality vs board accuracy.

Use Training Mode for practice. Flip on Recovery Mode before class to rehearse real consequences.
