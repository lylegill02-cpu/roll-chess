Hybrid Competition Rules

← Home

Effective: June 2026 · Format: Alternating mat rounds and chess games with linked consequences

1. Overview

ROLL CHESS is a hybrid competition where mat performance directly modifies chess conditions. Competitors alternate between a Mat Phase (timed BJJ sparring or positional rounds) and a Board Phase (standard chess, rapid or blitz per event tier).

2. Equipment

  • Standard chess set and clock (digital recommended)
  • BJJ gi or no-gi per event rules
  • Mat area and board area separated but visible to both competitors
  • Optional: ROLL CHESS spectrum tracker (trainer app or official score sheet)

Most board points (wins = 1, draw = 0.5 each). Tiebreakers: aggregate spectrum, combined mat DQS, Armageddon blitz.
